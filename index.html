02 · Product Gaps & Recommended Fixes

Five gaps, ordered by priority and ease

Based on 30+ minutes of hands-on use across all three modules, combined with EdTech market research [3][4][6].

Projected completion rate lift per fix
Estimated impact index (0–100) vs. implementation effort in days
Hindi UI: 95, Q-banks: 72, ATS feedback: 58, Streaks: 45, WhatsApp: 38
High priority · 0–30 days
Hindi / Regional-Language-First UI
AI Tutor & CV feedback are 100% English. Cell C/F drop-off trigger #1 [6]. Fix: Hindi default on mobile with language toggle at sign-up.
Impact: 2.3× session completion [6]
High priority · 30–60 days
Domain-Specific Interview Q-Banks
Generic HR questions regardless of stream. A BCom/Finance student needs very different prep from a CS student. Fix: stream-tagged Q-banks across Finance, Commerce, IT, Core Engineering.
Impact: Primary WTP driver [Ken Research 3]
Medium priority · 30–45 days
CV Feedback — Recruiter's Eye View
Builder gives feedback without explaining why, or showing what a recruiter sees. Fix: before/after ATS score toggle showing how the CV ranks in applicant tracking.
Impact: Top freemium→paid driver [Nexdigm 4]
Medium priority · 45–60 days
Progress Dashboard & Streak Mechanics
No visible cross-module progress tracker. No habit formation loop. Fix: 7-day free streak before paywall + daily push notification.
Impact: PhysicsWallah retention model proven [4]
Lower priority · 60–90 days
WhatsApp Onboarding Flow for Cell F
For first-gen, low-data users a web app is high-friction. Fix: conversational WhatsApp bot (free tier) adapting AI Tutor lessons to drive app sign-ups organically. Duolingo India's WhatsApp pivot boosted DAU by 38% [3].
Impact: Unlocks Cell F · ~14M additional students

03 · Go-To-Market Strategy

Cell C priority — organic-first, Hindi-first

Target: ~4.2M Tier-3 Engineering & MBA students in Hindi-belt cities (UP, Bihar, Rajasthan, MP). Est. addressable base rising to 18M with Cell F inclusion.

Channel & Message
YouTube Shorts + Instagram Reels in vernacular Hindi
  • Tagline: "Apna CV khud banana seekho — 10 minute mein"
  • 30-sec Reel: student opens pehchaan.me live, builds a CV, AI feedback shown in real time
  • CTA: "Try free at pehchaan.me" — no paid promotion
  • Creator DM outreach only: micro-influencers 10K–100K followers, Hindi EdTech niche
  • Cell C users spend 4.2 hrs/day on short-video platforms [IAMAI 2024, cited 3]
Why it works — proven benchmarks
Every comparable has done this exact playbook
  • Edureka: 60% of users acquired via free YouTube content [4]
  • PhysicsWallah: Hindi channel hit 10M subs before any paid media
  • Duolingo India Hindi pivot: +38% DAU, 6.2% free-to-paid conversion [3]
  • Vedantu organic SEO: 8.5M monthly visitors, 95% organic [4]

THE ONE CHANGE
Make Hindi the default language for all three modules on mobile, with a language toggle at sign-up. This single change unlocks Cell C and Cell F simultaneously — an addressable base of ~18 million Tier-3 students. Sadavar & Shaikh [6] document 2.3× higher session completion for regional-language EdTech. Ken Research [3] identifies Hindi-medium delivery as the #1 whitespace in AI-powered career tools. For Priya specifically: if the AI Tutor spoke Hindi, she would use it every day.
